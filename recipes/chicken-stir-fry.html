Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

Chicken stir-fry is a quick, colorful, and flavorful dish packed with fresh vegetables and bold seasoning. It’s a perfect balance of tender chicken and crisp veggies, making it both nutritious and satisfying. This dish is ideal for a fast meal that doesn’t sacrifice taste.

The sauce brings everything together with a savory and slightly sweet flavor, coating each ingredient evenly. With its vibrant mix of textures and simple cooking method, chicken stir-fry is a go-to recipe for busy days.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces.
  2. Chop all vegetables.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  4. Cook the chicken until browned.
  5. Add garlic and ginger.
  6. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  7. Pour in soy sauce and mix well.
  8. Serve hot.
